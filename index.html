Welcome to Livingstone and Zinga’s

We offer affordable accommodation in a friendly atmosphere. Whether you want to party, relax in a hammock in the garden or cool down in the swimming pool, this is the place for you! Our hostel, situated an easy walk from the city centre, provides a place for tourists and locals to interact and have fun together or learn from each other’s stories.

We can

  • book all the local activities available,
  • arrange safaris to national parks, as well as
  • assist you with onward travel.

If you want to do something good for the local community and really experience Zambian daily life during your time in Livingstone, check out our volunteer section!

Accomodation

Situated in close proximity to the city centre, we offer affordable accommodation (camping, dorms and private rooms) in a friendly atmosphere.

Activities

Livingstone has a lot to offer, don’t leave without visiting Victoria Falls and rafting the mighty Zambezi River!

Volunteering

By volunteering and participating in projects in Zambia, you are joining a community of local people and fellow volunteers.
